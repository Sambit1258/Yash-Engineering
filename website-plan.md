## Section 5 — Manufacturing Process

**Layout:** Horizontal step/timeline or numbered steps with icons

**Steps:**
1. **Raw Material Procurement** — Quality billets and coils sourced from approved vendors
2. **In-House Processing** — Rolling, cutting, and shaping as per specifications
3. **Quality Inspection** — Dimensional and tensile strength checks at each stage
4. **Surface Treatment** — Cleaning, finishing, and protective coating if required
5. **Packaging** — Secure bundling and labelling for safe transit
6. **Dispatch** — Timely loading and delivery to customer location
