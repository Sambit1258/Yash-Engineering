About Us

Built on Trust,
Driven by Quality

9+ Years of Excellence

Yash Engineering & Trading is a Nagpur-based manufacturer and trader of precision steel products serving industries across Maharashtra and pan-India. Founded with a commitment to quality and reliability, we have grown into a trusted name in the steel supply chain.

Our in-house manufacturing capabilities allow us to produce MS Channels, Bolts & Fasteners, and Mild Steel Strips that conform to BIS and IS standards — ensuring every product that leaves our facility meets the exact specifications our clients demand.

  • Family-driven business with founder-led quality oversight
  • In-house manufacturing — rolling, cutting & shaping under one roof
  • BIS / IS certified raw materials and finished products
  • Strong supplier & dealer network across Maharashtra & pan-India
